You Need A Parser

YNAP converts CSV files from a variety of sources into a format that can easily be imported into You Need A Budget. Just drag the files you want to convert into this window. Your files will never leave your browser.

Drag files here to parse

YNAP supports 39 different formats for banks of 15 countries, including Outbank, N26, ING-DiBa, comdirect, Kontist, Volksbank, easybank credit card, Raiffeisen Bank, Raiffeisen VISA, KBC checking, and more.
